This home insurance policy provides coverage for the following:

- Fire and smoke damage
- Lightning strikes
- Theft and vandalism
- Windstorm and hail
- Water damage caused by burst pipes
- Liability for injury occurring on the property

The following are NOT covered unless special riders are added:
- Earthquake damage
- Flooding caused by natural disasters
- Mold or rot
- Sewer backup
- Damage due to war or nuclear hazard

Deductible is $1000 per claim unless specified.
